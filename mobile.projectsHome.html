Muscle Music

Image

This Project started my interest in music. I used two muscle sensors placed on any muscle on the body, the sensors would read a value anywhere from 0-1024 and based on the value it read, it would play a tone based on the value being read. a value of 0 would play a 20Hz tone and a 1024 value would play a 20,000Hz tone
