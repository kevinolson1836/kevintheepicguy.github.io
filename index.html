My story

I am a new graduate from Illinois State University with a degree in Information Technology. I love to teach myself new skills within the technology sphere. I am always keeping up with new technologies that I can utilize to improve my skill set.


I have spent most of my free time developing projects for fun and to expand my knowledge. Some of the projects I am most proud of are the 2D Platformer I made in collaboration with my friends, my website (that you are currently on!), and my AirMouse Project. A full write-up on each project is listed below if you want more detail. I have independently developed numerous new skills about software development through my initiative and going beyond what was needed.


Development is not the only hobby that I have tinkered with. In addition to administering my own home Linux server, I have experience configuring other services such as a file server, HomeAssistant, VPN, and more. I have also been working with Windows Server 2019 to boost my experience with new systems. I initially had zero experience with Linux, but over the past two years I have put in the hard work and have gotten the hands-on experience to vastly improve my skills. I am now very confident in my ability to adapt to, and navigate, any Linux system.


During the summer of 2022, I completed an Internship at Advocate Aurora Healthcare, a hospital system with 26 hospitals. As a project manager, I participated in meeting, planned out future system upgrades, and I managed the structure of the file storage system. I also provided remote and in-person technical support for the newly installed hardware/software. During my time in college, I worked at Costco Wholesale. During this time, my reviews stated that I excelled in customer service and demonstrated effective communication with both customers and management. I was described as a hard worker with good time management skills.



In the future I plan to be an effective software Engineer -- always learning, adapting to, and mastering emerging technologies.
